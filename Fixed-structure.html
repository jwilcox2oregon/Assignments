Stop one on the west coast mountain tour: Mt. Shasta!

The tour of selected significant mountain peaks near the west coast begins in northern California. Take I-5 and exit at the aptly named town of Mt. Shasta.

Stop two on the west coast mountain tour: Mt. Hood!

Oregon's biggest mountain is stop number two. Continue north on I-5 toward the Portland area, then take I-205 and then Hwy 26 and you'll be at the peak in no time.

Stop three on the west coast mountain tour: Mt. Rainier

Feel free to chose your route making it to the last stop. You can back track to I-5 for more civilization or take a more nature heavy route, but either way we're heading north to Washington's biggest mountain.
